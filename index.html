Complicated tasks made superSimple

osxroot is the deep dark coder side of mac007.com, an OS X Server consulting firm. In addition to scripts to simplify managing OS X, osxroot also offers step by step tutorials for the non technical do it yourselfer.

superScripts are mostly written for OS X 10.6 and higher. Some may only work on certain versions and we will notate these to the best of our ability. Please use, enjoy and feel free to contribute and improve them. We love seeing how other coders think.
